Sarah runs several things at once, and they all point the same way: careers, freelancing and getting people into the right room. With over twenty years in recruitment behind her, she sponsored the first event through Extra Life, her course for freelancers, then decided that standing at the side of it was not going to be enough. She now negotiates the sponsorship, runs attendee engagement, and campaigns for the whole thing on social.
